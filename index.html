About Me

A Bit About Me

I am James Cole. I am currently studying Computer Systems Networks at NTU. I am a keen piano and bass guitar player with a passion for the flamboyant. I am a very people focused person and I aim to please everyone I work with. I work very hard but don’t forget that there is more to life than working. I know when to be sensible and when I can let my hair down (metaphorically). As part of my degree I have learnt how to work in a team and as an individual, so I feel very comfortable going into most situations in my professional career.

University

As I previously said I am studying Computer Systems Networks at Nottingham Trent University. In my first year I was awarded the Dan Ryland Prize for the most improved student in the whole school of science and technology. I have learnt many new programming languages as part of my university course, and I plan on expanding my knowledge of Java as it is my preferred programming language at the moment.
