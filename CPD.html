About The Website
This website was written as part of a university module and I had to basically use it as a way of documenting my activities in the first term at university. Where it differs from this website is the fact I was strictly limited to HTML and CSS and I could not really use any other form of styling or interactivity. As a result, it perhaps isn’t a true reflection of what sort of web page I can actually produce however as a first real go at web development I don’t think its an overly bad way to start.
